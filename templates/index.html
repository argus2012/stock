INTRODUCTION

There are so many factors involved in the stock market trend. Predicting how the stock market will perform is one of the most difficult things to do. This stock prediction system is designed to produce BUY or SELL results on S&P 500 tickers using Machine Learning to analyze the historical data and future predictions in the stock market.

Disclaimer & Risk Disclosure:
Please note that this project is only used for educational purposes of Machine Learning courses in the university. The result of machine learning is a summary of historical experience, and there is a possibility of failure. Investment is risky, you need to be cautious when entering the market.
